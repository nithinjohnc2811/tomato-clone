Are you brave enough to enter the God of the Sea's lair? This Trapdoor Drop Slide starts in a transparent capsule high above the park. A whimsical, hand-painted masterpiece for the whole family. Instead of horses, hop aboard giant seahorses, glowing jellyfish, and majestic sea turtles. Type:This is a land ride

Thrill Level: Low/Family-Friendly
