RIDE 1:The Shoreline Shaker

Experience the power of the tides without getting a drop of water on you! This high-intensity Airtime Thriller hurls riders 150 feet into the air before plunging them into a series of stomach-churning "bunny hops" and 90-degree banked turns

Type:This is a land ride

Thrill Level: Extreme

Best For: Adrenaline junkies who love "airtime."

RIDE 2:Neptune’s Revenge

Are you brave enough to enter the God of the Sea's lair? This Trapdoor Drop Slide starts in a transparent capsule high above the park. Catch your breath before the floor drops out, sending you into a near-vertical freefall that transitions into a translucent loop-the-loop. You’ll be a blur of speed until you hit the final massive splashdown.

Type:This is a water ride

Thrill Level: Extreme

Best For: 360-degree horizontal loop."

RIDE 3:The Coral Carousel

A whimsical, hand-painted masterpiece for the whole family. Instead of horses, hop aboard giant seahorses, glowing jellyfish, and majestic sea turtles. As the carousel spins, a custom orchestral soundtrack of "underwater" melodies plays, and hidden bubble machines create a magical, immersive atmosphere.

Type:This is a land ride

Thrill Level: Low/Family-Friendly

Best For: Young explorers and those looking for a nostalgic photo op."

RIDE 4:The Manta Ray Racer

Grab a mat and challenge your friends to the ultimate seafloor sprint! This Multi-Lane Mat Racer features four side-by-side enclosed tubes that twist and turn in the dark before opening up into a wide, downhill "wave" finish. Check the digital scoreboard at the bottom to see who took the gold!

Type:This is a land ride

Thrill Level: Medium

Best for:Competitive head-to-head racing
